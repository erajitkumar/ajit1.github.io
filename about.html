CodeChaf is an innovative high quality service to help you improve your performance in school as well as college, and can be used as studying materials.You have full flexibility to determine when and where to use CodeChaf as long as you are connected to the Internet. The material on CodeChaf is developed by experienced teachers who are passionate about teaching and to use new technologies. All material on CodeChaf follow the new curriculum for latest technology. All your progress and activities are saved allowing you to monitor your progress.  Your results and activities can also be used for feedback to your parents or your teacher.  There is no advertising or communication between students at CodeChaf - this is to create an environment that is solely focused on your learning and improve your results.
